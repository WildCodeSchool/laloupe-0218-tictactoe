This site was made by Laurent Lucas, Alexandra Lee and Paul Morea, web developers in training at WILD CODE SCHOOL (La Loupe, France). We used an ensemble of Google tools, namely, Angular 5 and Cloud Firebase to create this game and stock our data.

Alexandra was formerly a law librarian in California. An avid tic tac toe player in her distant childhood, today she enjoys tennis, baking, and riding horses. However, these days she spends her free time learning coding with the goal to make blockchains for lawyers in the near future.

Paul is 75% Norman and 25% Italian. His two passions are new technology and garden gnomes. When he was a little boy he loved to play tic tac toe. Today his favorite hobby is Aqua-Poney. But seriously, when he grows up he would like to develop awesome websites and travel in space.
