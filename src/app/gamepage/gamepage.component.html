SCOREBOARD RULES
The Rules
X's always go first. The object is to get three in a row. One player is known as X's and the other is O's. Players alternate placing their X's and O's on the game board until either player has three in a row, or all nine squares are filled. In the event that no one has three in a row, the stalemate is called a cat game.
